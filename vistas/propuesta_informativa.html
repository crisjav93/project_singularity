Building the Future

we do R&D differently

Singularity Labs is the R&D division dedicated to “building the future.” Our mission is to identify, develop, and bring transformative technologies to market that will have a long-lasting impact on people and the planet.

Science Creating Solutions

The core of this Action Plan is to promote projects and programs of critical importance to science and society. All of Singularity Labs' operations, whether in strengthening member engagement, building new partnerships, expanding its global reach through better communications, or mobilizing new resources through stronger fundraising. strategic, they aim to support the successful conduct of these scientific activities and maximize their impact.

Adaptive VR training platform

Training VR is a platform of training capsules that intends to create a Virtual Reality classroom in order to train and prepare students and technicians in their specialised fields. Through Virtual Reality, the intention is to recreate facilities and train students in a virtual environment where they can improve their abilities and capabilities, without the dangers of real facilities.
